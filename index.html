101 Art Studio

Located in Greater Boston Area, 101 Art Studio is the place where you can learn and play around with art. Art heals and develops human’s artistic minds. It provides a variety of art activities for adults and kids to improve their creativity. Our team is dedicated to art education, focusing on k-12 strategies and unit plans.
